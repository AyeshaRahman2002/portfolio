Biography

Born and raised in India, Dubai, and Saudi Arabia, I embarked on my academic journey by pursuing a Master of Engineering in Computer Science with Artificial Intelligence (2024-25). My fascination with technology began at a young age, but it was during my undergraduate years (2021-24) that I discovered my passion for applying AI to solve real-world problems. At the University of Leeds, I gained hands-on experience working on innovative projects ranging from high-frequency trading systems to sentiment analysis for stock market predictions. Each project not only sharpened my technical skills but also taught me the importance of teamwork, precision, and problem-solving in dynamic environments.

My interest in finance led me to explore the convergence of AI and the financial markets. I realized that the future of finance lies in leveraging data-driven insights, and I became determined to specialize in AI for market predictions. This led to my participation in multiple research-driven projects, where I combined sentiment analysis with neural networks to create predictive models that could potentially impact investment strategies. I have also gained onsight experience working for investment firms allowing me to combine my knowledge and skills in both Artificial Intelligence and Finance field.

Apart from academia, I’ve also earned several certifications in blockchain, finance, and software development, which have further strengthened my understanding of cutting-edge technologies. My thirst for knowledge drives me to stay updated with the latest developments in AI, finance, and design, as I constantly look for new challenges that push my boundaries.

In the coming years, I aim to bring my skills and knowledge to innovative companies where I can continue merging my expertise in AI with the ever-evolving financial landscape. Whether it's through predictive algorithms, building efficient systems, or optimizing processes, I look forward to contributing to shaping the future of technology and finance.
